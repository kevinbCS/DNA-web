DNA MARKETING

DNA Marketing is a new breed of marketing agency. We believe that
brands and companies should be able to reach their target audience
without the exorbitant rates of full-service agencies. DNA Marketing
was founded by veteran influencer and actor, Christian DelGrosso with
a vision that “reach” should, and must, pertain to the target audience
of brands, without reaching into their bank accounts.

Influencers are individuals who have the power to affect purchase
decisions of others because of their authority, knowledge, position
and/or relationship with their audience. DNA Marketing utilizes the
reach of the top influencers from around the world to deliver our
clients a measurable results.

We work with both Fortune 500 companies as well as early stage
companies who are eager to build their brand and loyal following.
Simply put, we help you do that. Welcome to DNA Marketing…..
